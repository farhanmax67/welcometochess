Openings & Tactics

The Ruy Lopez, also called the Spanish Opening, is one of the oldest and most popular chess openings. Moves: 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bb5. White aims to pressure Black's knight and control the center.

Key ideas:

  • Control the center with pawns and knights.
  • Prepare to castle king-side quickly.
  • Apply pressure on Black's knight at c6, indirectly threatening the e5 pawn.

The Art of the Opening

Control the center, develop pieces, and castle early. Understanding the 'why' behind opening moves is more important than memorizing lines. Common openings like the Ruy Lopez provide solid development.
