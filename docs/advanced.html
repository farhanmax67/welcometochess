Deep Positional Play

Strategic Thinking

Advanced strategy involves evaluating pawn structures, king safety, and long-term planning. You must understand space, piece activity, and initiative. Prophylaxis—stopping your opponent's plans—is a hallmark of master play.

The Technical Endgame

Mastering endgames like King and Pawn or Rook vs Pawn separates masters from experts. Every square matters, and calculating precise variations is required for conversion.
