Solve chess puzzles to improve your tactical awareness and pattern recognition.

Puzzle 1: Find the Fork

Chess Puzzle Position

[Interactive: Chess puzzle board]

Challenge: White to move. Find a move that attacks two pieces at once!

Hint: Look for a knight move that creates multiple threats.

Solution: Nf5! This fork attacks both the rook and the bishop.

Puzzle 2: Pin and Win

Chess Puzzle Position

[Interactive: Chess puzzle board]

Challenge: White to move. Use a pin to win material!

Hint: Look for a way to pin a piece to the king.

Solution: Bb5! Pins the knight to the king, winning the knight.

Puzzle 3: Checkmate in Two

Chess Puzzle Position

[Interactive: Chess puzzle board]

Challenge: White to move. Can you find checkmate in two moves?

Hint: Look for ways to limit the king's escape squares.

Solution: Qh7+ Kf8 Qh8# (or similar sequence depending on position).
