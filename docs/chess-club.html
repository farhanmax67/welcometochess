Chess Club

Join our vibrant chess community! Weekly sessions, friendly competition, and collaborative learning await.

Purpose of the Club

We run the club to give every student a place to learn, practice, and grow. Chess is our tool to build critical thinking, decision-making, patience, focus, teamwork, leadership, and sportsmanship.

Life Skills: We connect every session to real-life skills‚Äîplanning, choosing wisely under time pressure, collaborating, and showing respect.

Session Structure

  • Beginner Group: 20 mins fundamentals, 20 mins guided games, 10 mins reflection on patience and focus.
  • Intermediate Group: 20 mins tactics/strategy, 20 mins sparring games, 10 mins review on decision-making and sportsmanship.
  • Mixed Play: Friendly games that emphasize teamwork, respect, and growth mindset.
